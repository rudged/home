Here's a list of past and upcoming science education conferences:

  • AAAS

    The next annual meeting will be held on 12-16 Feb 2015 in San Jose, CA. (Meetings are generally held in early February. The submission deadline for general and student posters is 25 Apr 2014.)

  • ACS

    The American Chemical Society will hold its 247th Annual Meeting in Dallas, TX from 16-20 Mar 2014. Proposals should be submitted using the On-line Abstract Submittal System (OASYS). Science education proposals should be submitted for consideration by the Division of Chemistry Education. (Select CHED to go to the symposia page and then select the symposium topic.)

  • ASTE

    The Association for Science Teacher Education 2015 conference will be held 7-10 Jan 2015 in Portland, OR. Conference proposals are generally due in mid July of the preceding year.

  • EARLI

    The 16th Biennial Conference of the European Association for Research in Learning and Instruction will be held in Nicosia, Cyrprus from 30 Jun-4 Jul 2014. The deadline for proposals was 19 Dec 2013.
