Descendants of John Rudge of Gloucester.

The following is a genealogy of a branch of the Rudge family that migrated to Brazil. It is based primarily upon a work entitled Album Genealógico De Famïlia Vergueiro being written by Claudio Fortes. His web site is located here. Inquries about it may be directed, in Portuguese, to him at "claudiofortes@jbcultura.com.br"
