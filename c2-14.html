Here's a photograph of Herman BREITKREUTZ. It bears the following inscription written by his niece, Laura Adell nee Breitkreutz COOK: "Beverly- this is your Great Uncle Herman Breitkreutz on your mother's father's side of the family." The woman in the picture is no doubt his wife, Hulda nee Wendt BREITKREUTZ.
