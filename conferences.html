Here's a list of past and upcoming science education conferences:

  • AAAS

    The next annual meeting will be held on 12-16 Feb 2015 in San Jose, CA. (Meetings are generally held in early February. The submission deadline for general and student posters is 25 Apr 2014.)

  • ACS

    The American Chemical Society will hold its 247th Annual Meeting in Dallas, TX from 16-20 Mar 2014. Proposals should be submitted using the On-line Abstract Submittal System (OASYS). Science education proposals should be submitted for consideration by the Division of Chemistry Education. (Select CHED to go to the symposia page and then select the symposium topic.)

  • ASTE

    The Association for Science Teacher Education 2015 conference will be held 7-10 Jan 2015 in Portland, OR. Conference proposals are generally due in mid July of the preceding year.

  • EARLI

    The 16th Biennial Conference of the European Association for Research in Learning and Instruction will be held in Nicosia, Cyrprus from 30 Jun-4 Jul 2014. The deadline for proposals was 19 Dec 2013.

  • ESERA

    ESERA

    The European Science Education Research Association will hold its 2015 conference in Helsinki, Finland. The next conference will be held in 2017.

  • ICNPSE The International Conference New Perspectives in Science Education Edition Three will take place 20 - 21 Mar 2014 in Florence Italy.

  • IHPST

    The Thirteenth International History, Philosophy and Science Teaching Conference will take place 22-24 July 2015 at Rio de Janiero, Brazil.

  • IHPST-LA

    The 3rd Latin American Conference of the International History, Philosophy and Science Teaching Group has been scheduled to take place at Santiago de Chile, Chile from 17-19 Oct 2014, under the coordination of Dr. Mario Quintanilla Gratia.

  • ISHPSSB

    The International Society for the History, Philosophy and Social Studies of Biology held its last biennial meeting 7-11 Jul 2013 in Montpellier, France. The next one will be held 5-10 Jul 2015 in Montreal, Canada.

  • MSTA

    The 2015 conference will be held February 26-28, 2015 in Grand Rapids, MI. (Submissions are generally due the preceding October.)

  • NABT

    The 2014 conference for the National Association of Biology Teachers will be held 12-15 Nov 2014 at the Cleveland Convention Center, Cleveland, OH.

  • NARST

    The 2014 conference for the National Association of Researchers in Science Teaching is taking place 30 Mar-2 Apr 2014 at the Wyndham Grand Hotel, Pittsburgh, PA. The 2015 conference will be held in Chicago, IL. Proposals are due in mid August of the preceding year.

  • NSTA

    Information on the next National Science Teachers Association National Convention which will be held 3-6 Apr 2014 in Boston, MA. The 2015 conference will be held 12-15 Mar 2015 in Chicago; the 2016 conference will take place 31 Mar Ð 3 Apr 2016 in Nashville, TN.

  • SABER

    The Society for the Advancement of Biology Education Research will hold its next meeting 17-20 Jul 2014 at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, MN. The deadline to submit proposals was 21 Mar 2014. (The call for proposals is generally made available in February preceding the conference.)

  • SSMA

    The School Science and Mathematics Association will hold its annual conference on 6-8 Nov 2014 at the Omni Hotel in Jacksonville, FL. The next conference will be held Oklahoma City, Oklahoma (2015).
