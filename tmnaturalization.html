Thomas Mitchell’s Declaration of Intent to Become US Citizen

"In City Court of Brooklyn. State of New York, Kings County, City of Brooklyn. SS, I Thomas Mitchell Do declare on oath, that it is my intention to become a Citizen of the United States; and so renounce for ever all allegiance and fidelity to any Foreign Prince, Potentate, State or Sovereignty whatever; and particularly to the Queen of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland. Sworn in open Court, this 19th day of October 1850 Thomas Mitchell..." (North Carolina Naturalization Records (Camin) - Thomas Mitchell New Hanover County 1850. Citizenship and Naturalization Records 1850-1853 New Hanover Co. CR 070 902.1)
