GENERAL ADVICE

There are many resources on the web to help you with your genealogical project, from general advice on how to do research to resources like this site devoted to particular surnames and locations. Cyndi's List is one of the best places to start if you need general assistance or help with other branches of your family.

Cyndi's List of Genealogy Sites on the Internet

You should also consider visiting the Morman's Family Search site, which gives you direct access to their extensive computer records (mostly US and UK):
